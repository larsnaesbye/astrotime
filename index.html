Welcome to Tempus.

Tempus was made for Distro Astro - Linux for Astronomers. It's an attempt to address one of the currently emulated shareware packages and replace them with free and native Linux functionality and a Qt interface.

Tempus is currently in alpha and needs a few features fixed before it is ready for release.
