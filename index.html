Welcome to Tempus.

Tempus, a simple astronomy clock app, was intended for Distro Astro - Linux for Astronomers and as a first step into learning the Qt APIs. It addresses one of the currently emulated shareware packages and seeks to replace it with free and native Linux functionality.

Tempus is currently in alpha and is expected to make its first release around November 1st, 2015.
